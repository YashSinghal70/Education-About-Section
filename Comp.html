Eligibility

The Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning courses at Skill Nest are designed to be accessible to a wide range of learners, from beginners to advanced professionals. No prior experience in AI or ML is required, making our training ideal for anyone with a basic understanding of programming and a passion for technology. Whether you're a student aiming to build a career in AI, an IT professional seeking to upgrade your skills, or someone from a non-technical background looking to transition into the field, our courses are tailored to meet your needs. Whether you're looking to upskill, reskill, or start fresh, Skill Nest offers the perfect entry point into the world of AI and ML.
